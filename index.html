Features About Get Started

Usable on public transport

Stucardent can be used on public transport. The cards stored on stucardent can be activated for usage on public transport. All you do is press activate and put your phone near the scanner and your good.

All cards in one place

Stucardent helps students and other people with losing their cards. Ever had the problem of losing a student card or public transport card? Stucardent solves that problem by storing student cards and public transport cards on your phone.

Completely free

Stucardent stores student cards and public transport cards and allows them to be used on public transport. All for free.

What is Stucardent?

Stucardent is an app that stores student and public transport cards and makes them available for use on public transport. So all cards will be in one app and will never get lost. The app is mainly meant for students but other people can also use it. Ontop of all that it is completely free to use. If you have any questions contact us below.
